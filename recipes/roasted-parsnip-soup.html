Easy, yet Tasty Parsnip Soup

Love to make this Dish in the week as its quick and somewhat healthy due to the lack of fat you wont be adding

Ingredients Needed!

  • 1 ½ lb (700g) parsnips
  • 1 onion peeled cut into 8 wedges
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 tsp mild curry powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt & pepper
  • 750ml Vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp butter

How to Make it?!

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F / 200°C
  2. Peel and chop the parsnips, then peel the onion and cut into 8 wedges and add them to the baking sheet along with the garlic cloves.
  3. Drizzle with the oil and sprinkle with the curry powder, cayenne, salt and pepper, then toss it all together to coat. Spread it out on the tray, but don’t overcrowd the vegetables. You may need two trays. Roast for 25 minutes or until soft.
  4. Remove some of the roasted parsnips for garnish, then put the rest of the roasted parsnips, onion and garlic into a blender (don’t overfill) along with the vegetable stock and butter. You may need to do this in batches. Whiz until smooth.
  5. Add it back to the pot and reheat.
  6. Serve garnished with the reserved roasted parsnips.
